EXIF able - Privacy Policy

The privacy of our website visitors is important to us.

All the image processing is done locally on your computer web browser, your image never reach our server, you can use the web browser debug console to verify this.

As a visitor to the exifable.com website:

  1. No personal information is collected
  2. No information such as cookies is stored in the browser
  3. No information is shared with, sent to or sold to third-parties
  4. No information is shared with advertising companies
  5. No information is mined and harvested for personal trends
  6. No information is monetized


This website uses Beam Analytics to collect some anonymous usage data for statistical purposes, they are a GDPR, CCPA, PECR compliant website analytics tool, you can check their privacy policy. The goal is to track overall trends in our website traffic. It is not to track individual visitors. All the data is in aggregate only. No personal data is collected.
